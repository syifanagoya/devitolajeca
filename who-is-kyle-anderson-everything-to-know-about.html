Kyle Anderson Career

In the first round of the 2014 NBA draft, Anderson was Selected with the 30th Overall pick by the San Antonio Spurs. He signed the Spurs on July 12, 2014, for the Las Vegas Summer League.

Anderson also had multiple Assignments with the Austin Spurs of the NBA Development League during his rookie season and named the D-League Performer of the week on February 9. However, he became a restricted free agent after the 2017-2018 season.

After the Spurs declined to match the offer sheet, Anderson signed a four-year contract worth $37.2 million with Memphis Grizzlies.

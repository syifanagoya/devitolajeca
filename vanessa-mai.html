Vanessa Mai is a German singer and an ex-member of the band Wolkenfrei. She has an active career as a singer since 2012 when she joined Wolkenfrei. Since then, she has released many hit songs such as Jeans, T-Shirt und Freiheit, Wolke 7, and In all deinen Farben. She has a strongly growing fanbase with more than 500k followers on Instagram.
